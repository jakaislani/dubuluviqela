    Prince Daniel, Duke of Västergötland is a member of the Swedish royal family who has previously served as a personal trainer and gym owner as well as ran a company called Balance Training. With his marriage to Crown Princess Victoria, he has been styled as “His Royal Highness Prince Daniel, Duke of Västergötland” and was granted the style Royal Highness, making him an official member of the Swedish royal family.

    Prince Daniel, Duke of Västergötland Facts

  • He was born on the same day Carl XVI Gustaf ascended the throne of Sweden. The following year, Prince Daniel was christened at Almby Church in January 1974.
  • His father has been a leader in the Forest Finnish community.
  • He has served in the Swedish Army at Hälsinge Regiment in Gävle.
  • Prince Daniel has been a patron of organizations like A Swedish Classic, The Healthy Generation Foundation, International Society of Nephrology, and More Organ Donation.
  • He has been bestowed with several foreign honors which include the Grand Cross of the Order of the White Rose of Finland, Grand Gwanghwa Medal of the Order of Diplomatic Service Merit, and Knight Grand Cross of the Order of Merit of the Italian Republic.
  • In March 2021, it was announced that Prince Daniel and his wife, Crown Princess Victoria had been tested positive for coronavirus.

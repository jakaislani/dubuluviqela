THD+N - Smartphone Audio Quality Testing

20 Jul 2024, 00:00

THD+N is a measure of the total harmonic distortion and noise compared to the signal. The lower the number, the less distortion and noise there is relative to the fundamental frequency. THD+N is measured by driving a 1 kHz sine wave at maximum volume. Because there is always some inherent background noise, the THD+N is almost always lowest at maximum output so that is used for the measurement. There are two results that we take from this: a sine wave and a FFT spectrum.

    A LIFESTYLE influencer shared her easy lasagna recipe for "lazy girls."

    She joked that the Italians weren't going to be happy but the recipe only required three ingredients.

    Valerie (@vrogers17) shared the recipe with over 440,000 TikTok followers.

    "Let's make lazy girl lasagna," she said.

    To motivate herself to cook, she poured herself a glass of wine and turned on some music.

    "Tonight is extra easy dinner, it's lazy girl lasagna and you only need three ingredients," she said.

    "You need 24 ounces of frozen ravioli, mozzarella cheese, and 24 ounces of marinara sauce."

    First, she preheated her oven to 400 degrees and greased up a baking dish.

    "I know all you Italian people are going to be upset over this, it doesn't always have to be done y'all's way," she joked.

    She spread out a layer of marinara sauce over the pan, then a layer of ravioli, and another layer of sauce.

    She added cheese to the first layer of the creation, another layer of ravioli, the rest of the sauce, and finally, a last layer of cheese.

    "Bake for 30 minutes covered and 15 minutes uncovered," she said.

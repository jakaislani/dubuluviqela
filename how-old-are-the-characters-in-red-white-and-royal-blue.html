    How Old Are The Characters In Red White And Royal Blue?

    Red White and Royal Blue characters ages are Alex Claremont Diaz (23) and Prince Henry (24). Alex is one year younger than Henry.

    Directed by Matthew Lopez, Red, White & Royal Blue is based on the 2019 romance novel of the same name by Casey McQuiston.

    Lopez co-wrote the film with Ted Malawer and made his feature film directional debut with this rom-com.

    The romantic comedy film premiered at the BFI IMAX in London on July 22, 2023, and was later released on Amazon Prime on August 11.

    In 2019, Amazon Studios announced the movie’s development with Greg Berlanti serving as a producer. Principal photography took place between June and August 2022 in England.

    The film follows the star-crossed, secret romance between the Prince of England, Henry, and the son of the President of the United States, Alex.

    In the movie, the long-time rivalry between the First Son of the United States and the Prince of England transforms into romance as they manage a public relations crisis after a heated disagreement.

    Red White And Royal Blue Characters Ages

    The characters ages in Red White and Royal Blue are Alex Claremont Diaz (23) and Prince Henry (24). Alex and Henry are the lead characters of the movie.

    Alex’s zodiac sign is Aries and his birthday is in late March, approximately three weeks after Henry’s. He is the beloved son of President Ellen Claremont and his wife, Senator Oscar Diaz.

    He is a part of the White House trio with his elder sister June Claremont-Diaz and Nora Holleran, the granddaughter of Vice President Mike Holleran.

    Like his parents, he shares a keen interest in politics and has a sharp, witty attitude. His deep care for his community sets the scene for a clash with Prince Henry when they first meet.

    Henry, the spare to the British royal throne, is third in the line of succession after his mother, Princess Catherine, his elder brother Prince Philip, and before his sister, Princess Beatrice.

    Devoted to representing his country, he approaches his responsibilities with a serious demeanor that might cause him to appear cold. However, he also has a softer side that he shows to Alex later on.

    In addition to Alex and Henry, the film showcases several other pivotal characters. Let’s delve into a glimpse of these individuals.

    • Senator Oscar Diaz -Alex’s father
    • Ellen Claremont -Alex’s mother and the first female president of the US- In the movie, she was 27 when she was elected to the state legislature
    • Zahra Bankston -the Deputy Chief of Staff of Ellen Claremont- She has been working with Ellen since around 2003 when Alex was just five
    • Nora Holleran -22 years old granddaughter of the Vice President of the United States
    • Princess Beatrice -Prince Henry’s younger sister- She is in her early 20s
    • Percy Okonjo -Prince Henry’s best friend
    • Shaan Shrivastava -Prince Henry’s equerry
    • Amy -Secret Service Agent

    Alex Claremont-Diaz

    Red White and Royal Blue Alex age is 23 years old. The First Son of the United States was born on March 27, 1998, to Oscar and Ellen.

    Alex dated Nora during the campaign trail when he was 17 and Nora was 18. In the book, he first saw Henry at 12, spotting a 13-year-old Henry featured on a J-14 magazine page.

    Unlike the novel, where the pair’s in-person meeting occurs at the 2016 Rio Olympics, the film depicts their initial encounter at a party on the inaugural night of the Melbourne Climate Conference.

    As the son of the president and a senator, Alex nurtures his political dreams. He aspires to become a senator, driven by his desire to create a meaningful and positive impact.

    Prince Henry

    Red White and Royal Blue Prince Henry age is 24 years old. He was born on March 12, 1997, to Princess Catherine and Arthur Fox.

    In the book, he is the third youngest after Prince Philip and Princess Beatrice, but in the film, he is older than Beatrice.

    Torn between his brother’s conventional royal responsibilities and his sister’s rebellious stance on the monarchy, Henry’s romance with Alex aids his self-discovery.

    Red White and Royal Blue Cast Ages

    Red White and Royal Blue cast ages are Taylor Zakhar Perez (31) and Nicholas Galitzine (28). The latter is three years younger than the former.

    Galitzine was born on September 29, 1994, in Kefalonia, Greece. Growing up, he played rugby and football and took part in various county-level athletics competitions.

    Prior to becoming an actor, he was a city financier. In 2014, he got his first role in the film The Beat Beneath My Feet, starring alongside Luke Perry.

    His Red, White & Royal Blue co-star, Perez, was born on December 24, 1991, in Chicago, Illinois. Growing up in Chesterton, Indiana, he attended Chesterton High School, where he was a swimmer.

    The actor is of Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and Mexican descent. He is the third youngest of total eight children of his parents.

    After turning a swimming scholarship to Fordham University, he joined UCLA, majoring in Spanish, Culture, and Community and minoring in TV and Films.

    Red White And Royal Blue Age Rating

    Red White And Royal Blue age rating is 12+ on Amazon Prime. This indicates that it is suitabille for viewers aged 12 and older. However, the rom-com doesn’t shy away from showing adult content.

    The Prime Video content advisory section reads that the movie contains nudity, alcohol use, smoking, foul language, and adult content.

    As the film is released exclusively on streaming platforms and did not have a theatrical run in UK cinemas, its rating is determined by Prime Video rather than by the British Board of Film Classification (BBFC).

    However, it’s worth noting that in March of this year, both organizations declared an “enhanced collaboration.”

    Interestingly, the movie has been given an R-rating by the Motion Pictures Association in the United States, indicating that viewers under the age of 17 should only watch it when accompanied by an adult.

    Why is Red White and Royal Blue rated R?

    Red White and Royal Blue is rated R because of its inclusion of strong language, certain mature themes, and instances of partial nudity.

    In light of these elements, parents are strongly encouraged to consider this rating when making viewing decisions for their children.

    Speaking to Attitude, director Matthew Lopez shared, “Part of me was surprised at the R-rating, and part of me wasn’t”.

    “I question if it had been a man and a woman, would we have got an R-rating for that scene? More importantly, if it had been a scene about violence, instead of love, would it have got an R rating or a PG 13 rating?” he added.
